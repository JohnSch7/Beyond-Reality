It's very exciting seeing all the new technologies of the digital world. We're blurring the line between reality and CGI, and it's getting harder to distinguish the two. It may seem intimidating to get involved in virtual production, so I'll list a few resources to help you get started. From websites, to tutorials, and references, I'll make sure to go over a variety of tools that will help you take your first step.
  1. UnrealEngine.com
    • The UnrealEngine website to learn more about its capabilities.
  2. ArtStation.com
    • ArtStation has an amazing community of artists that share their projects and workflows.
  3. My 3D Inspiration Playlist
    • This is my YouTube playlist filled with 3D projects made in Blender, Unreal Engine, Houdini, etc. It also contains behind-the-scenes breakdowns.
  4. Unreal Sensei's Unreal Engine 5 Beginner Tutorial - UE5 Starter Course
    • In this video, Unreal Sensei teaches you how to install Unreal Engine, navigate the 3D viewport, create materials and worlds, sculpt landscapes, paint foliage, program in Unreal using Blueprints, and end with creating a photorealistic world in real-time.
  5. Cinecom.net's Ultimate Beginners Guide to Virtual Production in Unreal Engine 5
    • In this video, the people behind Cinecom.net teach you how to link a physical camera to a virtual camera, set up a virtual scene in Unreal Engine, record your talent in front of a green screen, and place them in your scene.
  6. Gabe Tandy's Create a Photorealistic Landscape in Unreal Engine 5
    • In this video, Gabe Tandy teaches you how to create a level within Unreal Engine, create, import, and texture the terrain, scatter foliage, set up lighting, post processing color grading, animations, and then render everything.
  7. pinkpocketTV's UE5 Live Link Face Motion Capture Tutorial using MetaHuman Characters
    • In this video, pinkpocketTV teaches you how to import a MetaHuman from Quixel Bridge into Unreal Engine, set up the connection from your mobile device to Unreal, then record your own facial motion capture data to the face of the MetaHuman.
  8. Travis Davids MetaHuman Creator - Basic Introduction - Create Realistic Digital Humans
    • In this video, Travis Davids teaches you the basics of navigating the MetaHuman Creator UI, facial modification settings, and how to export your character to Quixel bridge.
  9. Williams Faucher's Capturing Lofoten in Unreal Engine 5
    • In this video, Williams Faucher explains how he uses photogrammetry to scan a cabin, turn it into a 3D asset, import it to Unreal Engine, light the environment, add mountains, foliage, and then render the scene.
