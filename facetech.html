MetaHuman Creator

One of the hardest aspects to get right when creating a digital character is the face. This includes sculpting a realistic model, texturing the skin, and animating all its features. It's notoriously difficult and time-consuming for even the most skilled 3D artists to create a convincing digital character. This is where Epic Games' MetaHumans come in. MetaHuman Creator allows artists to make their own digital characters with ease.

MetaHuman Creator comes with presets and selectable styles for each part of the face. There's also a sculpting mode which allows artists to redefine desired areas of the face. Real-time updates let artists see their changes instantly which allows constant adjustments to be made. Once the character creation phase is finished, artists can export their model to Unreal Engine to animate and render the MetaHuman.

MetaHuman Animator

MetaHuman Animator is a newer feature made by Epic Games. It captures the facial performance of an actor and maps the motion capture data to the face of a MetaHuman in Unreal Engine. In the video demonstration linked above, Melina Juergens does a live performance in front of a phone using the Live Link Face mobile app developed by Epic Games.

MetaHuman Animator uses video and depth information to convert the performance into a high-fidelity animation. It can also analyze the audio to produce convincing tongue animations. A MetaHuman that resembles Melina's facial features is generated by using the video from her performance. This is referred to as her MetaHuman DNA which also generates a rig that can predict all of her facial expressions. Once the video processing is finished, the motion capture data and MetaHuman model can be dropped into the Unreal Engine timeline and rendered.
